
### Fluid Simulator
A simple grid-based Eulerian fluid simulator.
[Web build](https://colouredcat.github.io/Fluid_Sim/web/sim.html)
Instructions:
Place the mouse at the edge of the canvas and press A and D to apply force
Press R to reset
